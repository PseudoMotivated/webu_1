Pawn of Prophecy by David Eddings

Synopsis

A young man named Garion has been living a completely innocous life in the honest land of Sendaria, until he wasn't.
One fateful day, on one of Wolfs regular visits his life is turned on its head as he is pulled into a journey chasing god knows what - literally.

About

Published March 12, 1982 by Del Rey as part of a five books series called the Belgiard.
